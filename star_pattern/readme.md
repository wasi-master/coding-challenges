# What is this project?

This project is to make a program that prints a star-like pattern on the console

## Goal

Print a star pattern like so:

```
      * 
     * *      
    * * *     
   * * * *
  * * * * *
 * * * * * *
* * * * * * *
 * * * * * *
  * * * * *
   * * * *
    * * *
     * *
      *
```
